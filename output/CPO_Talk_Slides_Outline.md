## DESIGN RECOMMENDATIONS

### Color Palette
- **Primary:** Methodology brand color (deep green or earth tone)
- **Secondary:** Neutral gray for text
- **Accent:** Bright color for highlights (orange, teal, or gold)
- **Negative numbers:** Red
- **Positive outcomes:** Green

### Typography
- **Headlines:** 48-60pt, bold, sans-serif
- **Body text:** 24-30pt, regular, sans-serif
- **Quotes:** 36-48pt, serif (for contrast)
- **Captions:** 18-20pt, light

### Transitions
- Keep transitions simple (fade or none)
- Use build animations for lists (reveal one item at a time)
- Avoid flashy effects (distracting)

### Images
- Use high-quality Methodology product photos
- Keep images to 1-2 per slide max
- Ensure photos are rights-cleared
