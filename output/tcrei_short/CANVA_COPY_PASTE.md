## ğŸ’¡ Quick Canva Shortcuts

### Creating Shapes:
1. Click "Elements" (left sidebar)
2. Search "rectangle rounded"
3. Drag to canvas
4. Resize and color

### Adding Gradients:
1. Select background
2. Click color
3. Choose "Gradient"
4. Select "Linear gradient"
5. Adjust colors and direction

### Duplicating Elements:
- Select element
- Press `Cmd+D` (Mac) or `Ctrl+D` (Windows)
- Perfect for repeating sections!

### Aligning Elements:
- Select multiple elements
- Click "Position" at top
- Choose "Align center" or "Distribute vertically"
