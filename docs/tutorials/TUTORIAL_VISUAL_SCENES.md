## üéØ Visual Style Guide

### Overall Aesthetic
- **Style**: Modern, clean cartoon/illustration style
- **Tone**: Professional but approachable
- **Color Palette**:
  - Primary: #4A90E2 (Blue - trust, technology)
  - Secondary: #50C878 (Green - success, growth)
  - Accent: #FF6B6B (Red - attention, errors)
  - Background: #F8F9FA (Light gray - clean)
  - Text: #2C3E50 (Dark gray - readable)

### Character Design
- **Main Character**: "Dev" - the developer
  - Age: 25-35 appearance
  - Style: Casual (hoodie or t-shirt, jeans)
  - Expression: Initially frustrated ‚Üí curious ‚Üí satisfied
  - Hair: Modern style, diverse representation
  - Accessories: Glasses optional, headphones around neck

- **Claude Representation**:
  - Style: Friendly AI assistant icon/avatar
  - Design: Circular avatar with subtle animation (breathing effect)
  - Color: Matches Claude's brand (orange/teal tones)

### Typography
- **Main Titles**: Montserrat Bold, 48-60pt
- **Subtitles**: Montserrat Regular, 32-40pt
- **Body Text**: Open Sans Regular, 24-28pt
- **Code**: JetBrains Mono, 20-24pt
- **Callouts**: Impact or similar bold sans-serif, 36pt

### Animation Principles
- **Transitions**: 0.3-0.5s smooth easing
- **Text**: Fade in with slight upward motion
- **Code**: Typing animation (fast but readable)
- **Icons/Graphics**: Pop in with subtle bounce
- **Scene Changes**: Slide or fade transitions

## üí° Pro Animation Tips

### Readability:
- Text on screen for at least 2 seconds minimum
- High contrast (dark text on light BG or vice versa)
- Avoid too much text at once (max 2-3 lines)

### Emphasis:
- Use scale/size for importance
- Color = meaning (green = good, red = error, blue = info)
- Animation draws eye (motion attracts attention)

### Consistency:
- Same Dev character throughout
- Consistent UI templates (don't switch editor styles)
- Maintain color palette across all scenes

### Performance:
- Keep animations simple and smooth
- Avoid laggy particle effects (use sparingly)
- Test on different devices (desktop, mobile)
