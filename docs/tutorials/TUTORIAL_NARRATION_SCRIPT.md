## üìù Pronunciation Guide for AI Voice

Input these phonetic spellings if your TTS mispronounces:

- **RPI**: "R P I" (spell out, not "rippy")
- **TCREI**: "T C R E I" (spell out each letter)
- **MAKER**: "MAY-ker"
- **CoVe**: "KOH-vee"
- **.claude**: "dot cloud"
- **schema.ts**: "schema dot T S"
- **.md**: "dot M D" or "dot markdown"
- **GitHub**: "git hub" (not "git-hub")
- **JWT**: "J W T" (spell out)
- **OAuth**: "oh-auth"
- **bcrypt**: "B crypt"
- **DeepMind**: "deep mind" (two words, equal stress)
